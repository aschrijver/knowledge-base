Other Notices

Reprinted with permission.

This book has been out of print and very hard to find for many years. For this reason, I decided to undertake the task of putting it into electronic media for wider dissemination. If you need to contact me about this book, please email me, Daniel Risacher, magnus@alum.mit.edu. However, I wish to reiterate that I am not the author of this book, nor am I an expert on rockets. This book was written by Leroy Krzycki, who, when I contacted him in 1995, had been retired for some time.

The book was scanned from a copy provided to me by the generosity and foresight of Wayne Gramlich of Sun. Numerous errors were introduced by the scanning and OCR process that I and other volunteers removed by hand. Special thanks goes to Jim Glass of Rocketdyne for his outstanding efforts.
